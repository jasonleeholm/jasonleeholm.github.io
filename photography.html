Editing

I once worked for an auto accessory company. The installer would take a picture of a truck or car in the parking lot with reflections of themselves and keys left in the door, and I had to clean up the photos and put them in a nice setting like a sunset or a mountain road. This is one of my favorite edits. In this case I had to digitally add the step bar to the truck as well.
