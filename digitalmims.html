The Mims

These characters are called Mims. They are little amphibian-like aliens that I've changed over time, but kept the basic idea that they represent different learning styles / multiple intelligences.

Back to Digital

The group was once called The MultiPals, and there were 9 of them.

Back to top

I built a model of them in 3D, and created 2D designs from the model.
